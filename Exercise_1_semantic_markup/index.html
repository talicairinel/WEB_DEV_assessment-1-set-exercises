Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade cookies with gooey chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

If you don't have eggs, you can use a suitable egg substitute like applesauce, mashed bananas, or yogurt. Use 1/4 cup of the substitute for each egg in the recipe.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter, sugars, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 8–10 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Let cool before transferring to wire racks.
